Troubleshooting Guide

If you're experiencing technical issues, try these steps:

  • 🔄 **Refresh the page** – This can resolve minor display errors.
  • 🧹 **Clear browser cache and cookies** – Improves loading speed and fixes login issues.
  • 🔐 **Check your login credentials** – Ensure your email and password are entered correctly.
  • 🔌 **Verify payment methods** – If transactions fail, check that your payment card details are up to date.

Order & Payment Issues

Common order-related problems and solutions:

  • 💰 **Payment declined?** – Double-check your payment method or contact your bank.
  • 📦 **Order not received?** – Check your tracking number on the **Order History** page.
  • 📞 **Need a refund?** – Visit the **Returns & Refunds** section in your account.

Account Management

Manage your account settings and privacy:

  • 🔑 **Forgot password?** – Reset it from the **Login** page.
  • ⚙️ **Update account info?** – Modify your details in **Account Settings**.
  • 🚫 **Want to delete your account?** – Contact support for assistance.
