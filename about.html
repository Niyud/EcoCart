About Eco-Cart

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: At Eco-Cart, we envision a future where sustainable shopping is accessible to all, enabling individuals to make environmentally responsible choices without compromising convenience or affordability.

Mission: Our mission is to create an eco-friendly marketplace that connects consumers with sustainable products. We strive to promote ethical sourcing, reduce waste, and encourage conscious consumerism through innovative solutions and responsible business practices.

Privacy & Policies

At Eco-Cart, we prioritize transparency and user privacy. Below are key principles of our privacy policy:

  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.
  • User data is securely stored and used only to enhance your shopping experience.
  • Our website utilizes encryption protocols to ensure secure transactions.
  • Customers have full control over their data, with options to manage and delete account information.

By using Eco-Cart, you agree to our responsible use of cookies and data collection for site functionality and personalization.
